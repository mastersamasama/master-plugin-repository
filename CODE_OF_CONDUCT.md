## Quick reference

While the authoritative policy lives at the link above, the short version is:

- **Be respectful.** Disagreements about code, approach, or design are fine. Personal attacks are not.
- **Assume good faith.** Reviewers are volunteering their time during a busy hackathon.
- **Confidentiality first.** This repo is OKX internal — do not share screenshots, PRs, or content externally.
- **No harassment.** Of any kind, in any channel — issues, PR comments, internal chat, hackathon events.
- **Compliance over cleverness.** If a clever submission violates internal IP, security, or compliance policy, it does not ship. No exceptions.

## Reporting

To report a Code of Conduct violation related to this repository:

1. Contact the hackathon organizers directly (internal channels)
2. Or escalate via the standard OKX HR / Compliance reporting path described in the linked policy
3. Or DM the repo maintainer (`@mastersamasama`) on internal chat

Reports are confidential. Retaliation against reporters is itself a Code of Conduct violation.

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- All activity in this repository (PRs, issues, comments, commits)
- Any communication channels (chat, email, video calls) related to hackathon submissions
- Hackathon events (in-person or virtual)
- Any external venues where you represent the hackathon or this marketplace

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:

- A private warning from the maintainer
- Removal of submission(s) from the marketplace
- Disqualification from the hackathon
- Escalation to OKX HR / Compliance per the linked policy

The maintainer's decisions are subject to override by OKX HR / Compliance per the linked policy.
